Who We Are

This catalog is maintained by the US Antarctic Program Data Center, based at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory of Columbia University.

What We Do

This catalog provides access to metadata for products of research funded by the National Science Foundation Antarctic Research program. Metadata records are harvested from the US Antarctic Program data center (http://usap-dc.org) and from the NASA Global Change Master Directory (GCMD). Antractic Master Direcory (AMD)
